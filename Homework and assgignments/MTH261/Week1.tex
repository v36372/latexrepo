\section*{Section 1.1 - Problem 15}
Use parametric equations to describe the linear system solution set.
\subsection*{a}
\begin{eqnarray*}
\ 2x -3y & = & 1\\
6x + -9y & = & 3\\
\end{eqnarray*}
Augmented matrix
\[
A 
=
\begin{bmatrix}
    2       & -3& 1 \\
    6       & -9 & 3 \\
\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}
    1       & -\frac{3}{2} & \frac{1}{2} \\
\end{bmatrix}
\]

\begin{eqnarray*}
\ x -\frac{3}{2}y & = & \frac{1}{2}\\
x & = & \frac{3}{2}y+\frac{1}{2}\\
x & = & \frac{3}{2}t +\frac{1}{2},\: y=t
\end{eqnarray*}
For example, \(t=0\) yields solution \((\frac{1}{2},0)\)
